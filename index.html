Trade Gold For Resources
Game Rules
# of Players Ages Time
3 - 6 12+ Varies

Gameplay here....

Players are able to trade resources during their turn

Trades are conducted on a 2 for 1 ratio, meaning it costs two resources for one of another resource

However, resources cannot be traded for one another, they have to be traded for Gold first then that Gold can be traded for the desired resource, also on a 2 for 1 ratio
